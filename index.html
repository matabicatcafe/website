Our Story

More Than Just a Cafe

Matabi began as a simple dream; one that started with a love for cats and a desire to create something warm, comforting, and a little different. What began as my mom's passion project slowly grew into a space where every detail, from the atmosphere to the personalities of our cats, was shaped with care. Even the name "Matabi" carries a quiet meaning for those who notice; a small nod to both curiosity and the joy of returning again.

Today, Matabi is more than just a cat cafe; it's a place to pause. Whether you're here to unwind after a long day or simply spend time surrounded by gentle company, we hope you'll find a moment of calm here. Each cat brings its own charm, each corner invites you to stay a little longer, and everything is designed to help you slow down, breathe, and just be.

Located in the heart of Legian, just steps from the beach, Matabi is your perfect Bali sanctuary — whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family seeking a unique Bali experience you'll talk about long after you leave.
